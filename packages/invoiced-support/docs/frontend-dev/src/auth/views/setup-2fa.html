Two-Factor Authentication

Two-factor authentication adds an extra layer of security to your Invoiced account by requiring you to enter a verification code from the Authy app on your mobile device each time you login.

Two-factor authentication ensures that no one (such as an overseas hacker) can access your Invoiced account without physical access to your mobile device.
