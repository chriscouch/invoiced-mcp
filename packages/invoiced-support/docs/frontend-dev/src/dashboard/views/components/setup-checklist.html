Setup Payments

Don't forget to let your customers know how to pay! With Invoiced you can get paid in a variety of ways, making it more convenient for customers.

Online Payments

You can accept instant, online payments with Invoiced, including credit and debit cards, ACH, direct debit, and PayPal. When you receive a payment online, Invoiced will mark the invoice as paid, automatically.

Payment Instructions

Customers still sending a check? No problem! We can display your payment instructions on invoices and the payment page. Your customers can even click a button to let you know when a check is in the mail (known as a promise to pay).

Sending Invoices

Once you have setup your business profile and enabled payments, you are ready to send your first invoice. If you have not already, you will need to create an invoice first.

Invoices can be sent via email, direct mail, and text message. In order to send your first invoice, you simply Send Invoice on an open invoice.
